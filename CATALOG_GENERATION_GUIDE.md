# Sri Kamatchi Jewellery: Advanced Catalog Generation Guide
## Professional 9:16 Showroom Assets for Digital Studio

This guide provides the exact technical specifications and prompts needed to generate a high-end, 40-image professional catalog.

---

## 1. Technical Specification (The "Gold Standard")
To ensure every image fits perfectly in your 9:16 Digital Studio:
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (Vertical)
*   **Composition:** **Dead Center.** The jewelry must be perfectly centered to allow your store's branding and rates to sit around it without covering the product.
*   **Best AI Model:** **Flux.1 [Pro]** (Recommended for realism and centering) or **Midjourney v6.1**.

---

## 2. The "Wow-Factor" Series (Premium Backgrounds) 🌟
Use these prompts for the most unique, high-end look that separates your shop from everyone else.

### The "Starry Obsidian" (Deep & Magical)
> **Prompt:** "Professional photograph of a 22K Gold necklace. **Strictly centered**, 9:16 ratio. **Background:** Placed on a polished solid **Black Obsidian slab** that has a natural 'starry night' mineral pattern inside it. The stone reflects the gold softly. Cinematic backlighting, sharp macro focus, breathtaking and unique."

### The "Ember Dust" (Warm & Luxurious)
> **Prompt:** "Commercial photo of 22K Gold Bangles. **Centered framing**, 9:16 ratio. **Background:** Suspended over a dark surface with thousands of **tiny glowing golden ember particles** floating in the soft-focus background. Create a warm, magical aura around the gold. Ultra-realistic, extremely attractive, 8k."

### The "Liquid Sun" Unique Choker
> **Prompt:** "Professional 8k photography of a unique 'Liquid Gold' Choker necklace. **Strictly centered**, 9:16 ratio. The design looks like molten 22K gold flowing into organic, water-drop shapes. Set against a background of dark volcanic sand. High-contrast lighting, shimmering reflections, ultra-modern and attractive, photorealistic."

## 3. Photorealistic Collection (Realistic / Non-AI Style)
Use these for a "real photo" look with professional camera settings.

### Real Shop Jewelry (Centered 9:16)
> **Prompt:** "Commercial catalog photography of a 22K Gold necklace. **Strictly centered**, 9:16 aspect ratio. Shot on a Sony A7R IV with a 90mm macro lens at f/8. **Lighting:** Professional off-camera flash with a beauty dish, creating realistic sharp reflections on the metal. **Background:** Neutral grey textured showroom wall. No CGI, no 3D render, no retouching, photorealistic, natural gold color, 8k."

### Realistic Macro Ring (Centered 9:16)
> **Prompt:** "Authentic professional photograph of a handmade 22K Gold ring. **Centered framing**, 9:16 ratio. Visible subtle metal textures and hallmark stamp. Natural studio lighting, soft shadows, 1:1 macro magnification. Sharp focus, high depth of field. No AI-style glow, realistic metal density, photorealistic."
